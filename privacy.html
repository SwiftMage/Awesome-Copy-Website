Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information when you use the Awesome Copy app.

Information We Collect

The Awesome Copy app itself does not collect or store any personal information directly from its users. All clipboard data managed by the app remains stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the core features of the app.

For subscription management, we use RevenueCat, a third-party service that processes purchase information. RevenueCat may collect certain identifying information such as user IDs, device information, and transaction details to manage your subscription status. This information is used solely for managing your subscription and providing access to premium features. For more information about how RevenueCat handles your data, please review their Privacy Policy.

Data Usage

Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not use it.

Any diagnostic or crash report data collected by Apple through the standard App Store mechanisms is anonymous and used solely for improving app stability and performance.

Information collected by RevenueCat is used exclusively for subscription management purposes, including verifying purchase status, providing access to premium features, and processing transactions. This data is handled according to RevenueCat's privacy policy and is necessary for the functioning of subscription features.

Children's Privacy

Awesome Copy is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any users, including children.

Third-Party Services

Awesome Copy uses RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat collects and processes transaction information and device identifiers necessary for subscription management. This information is governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Standard Apple services for crash reporting and analytics may collect anonymized data as per Apple's policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
